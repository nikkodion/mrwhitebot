**Commands:**

`!quote` - generate a random quote from the Revue Starlight movie.
`!mrwhite` - send a picture and it will become mr white
`!revuegif` - send a random GIF from Tenor with the keywords 'revue starlight'
`!gm` - send a random Blingee good morning Revue Starlight GIF
`!ga` - send a random Blingee good afternoon Revue Starlight GIF
`!gn` - send a random Blingee good night Revue Starlight GIF
`!shitpostchar` - randomly generates a Revue Starlight meme with a random gif from Tenor with a character's name
`!shitpostquote` - same as above except it uses a quote from the Revue Starlight movie

The bot will also sometimes randomly reply to user messages with either a Revue Starlight movie quote or a character's image with a speech bubble.
